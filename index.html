Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our healthcare services, appointments, doctors, and patient care. If you have any additional questions, please feel free to contact our clinic.

  • How can I book an appointment?

    You can book an appointment by contacting our clinic through phone, visiting the clinic directly, or using our online appointment request service.

  • What medical services do you provide?

    We provide general medical consultations, health checkups, diagnosis, treatment for common illnesses, preventive care, and healthcare guidance for patients of all ages.

  • What are the clinic opening hours?

    Our clinic is open during regular working hours. Please contact us to confirm doctor availability and appointment schedules.

  • Do you provide emergency medical care?

    We provide medical assistance for urgent health concerns. For serious emergencies, patients should contact emergency services immediately.

  • Do you accept walk-in patients?

    Yes, walk-in patients are welcome. However, booking an appointment in advance is recommended to reduce waiting time.
